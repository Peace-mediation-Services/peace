About OPMS

Our mission and vision for a peaceful world

Who We Are

The Organization for Peace, Mediation and Services (OPMS) is a non-governmental organization dedicated to establishing sustainable peace through mediation and providing essential services in education, healthcare, and humanitarian aid.

Our Mission

To promote peace through conflict resolution and mediation while improving quality of life through educational programs, healthcare services, and humanitarian assistance.

Our Vision

A world where conflicts are resolved peacefully and all people have access to basic needs and opportunities for growth.
